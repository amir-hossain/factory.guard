# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

Act as a Senior Business Analyst and Product Consultant specializing in Manufacturing and Smart Factory Software.

Create a comprehensive Business Requirements Document (BRD) for a SaaS-based Smart Factory Incident & Action Management Platform designed for Garment Manufacturing Factories.

## Business Background

Most garment factories currently manage operational incidents through disconnected channels:

* Machine breakdowns are reported through WhatsApp groups.
* Quality issues are tracked in Excel spreadsheets.
* Safety incidents are communicated via phone calls.
* Compliance findings are shared through email chains.
* There is no centralized visibility of incident status.
* Management lacks analytics and trend reporting.
* Corrective actions are difficult to track and often become overdue.

The organization wants to digitize and standardize incident management across factory operations.

## Business Objectives

The platform should:

* Centralize all incident reporting.
* Improve accountability through action tracking.
* Reduce incident resolution time.
* Enable root cause analysis.
* Automate escalation processes.
* Improve compliance management.
* Provide real-time operational visibility.
* Generate actionable analytics and trends.
* Support multiple factories and departments.

# Incident Management Module

The platform must support:

### Incident Creation

* Report incidents from web and mobile devices.
* Upload images and attachments.
* Capture incident title and description.
* Select category.
* Select severity level.
* Tag factory, building, floor, line, and department.
* Record date and time.
* Capture reporter information.
* Generate unique incident ID automatically.

### Incident Categories

Include at minimum:

* Machine Breakdown
* Quality Issue
* Safety Incident
* Compliance Finding
* Production Delay
* Utility Failure
* Environmental Incident
* Other

### Severity Levels

* Low
* Medium
* High
* Critical

### Incident Workflow

Open → Under Review → Investigation → Action In Progress → Pending Verification → Closed

Include status transition rules.

# Corrective Action Management

Each incident can have one or multiple corrective actions.

Each corrective action should contain:

* Action title
* Description
* Assigned owner
* Due date
* Priority
* Department
* Status
* Completion percentage
* Evidence attachment

### Corrective Action Workflow

Assigned → In Progress → Review → Completed → Verified

Include validation rules and approval requirements.

# Escalation Engine

Define business rules for:

### Severity-Based Escalation

High Severity:

* Notify Supervisor immediately.
* Escalate if not acknowledged within 2 hours.

Critical Severity:

* Notify Department Manager immediately.
* Escalate to Factory Manager within 30 minutes.

### SLA Escalation

* Flag overdue actions.
* Send reminders before due dates.
* Escalate unresolved incidents.
* Escalate actions exceeding SLA.

### Notification Channels

* In-App Notification
* Email
* SMS
* WhatsApp Integration (Future Phase)
